2. Nelson-Siegel factors over time¶

  • beta1 (Level): long-run rate anchor
  • beta2 (Slope): short-end premium — negative means inverted curve
  • beta3 (Curvature): medium-term hump — captures policy uncertainty

3. Slope factor (beta2) — the ARCM primary signal¶

beta2 is the core input to regime detection and OU calibration.

  • Prolonged negative beta2 during ZLB era (2009-2015): mean-reversion breaks down
  • Sharp negative excursion during 2022-2023 hiking cycle
  • Current reading (+10.77%) well above tightening regime mean (+8.44%)
